CORE Filtration is a manufacturers representative organization for 3 major filtration media and equipment manufacturers in the US. As exclusive agent in the northeast for Franklin Products Inc., Newark Wire Cloth Co. and Filtrox NA, Core can offer a complete line of products from coarse screening to sub-micron filtration for the Food, Life Sciences, Chemical and Environmental Industries. Core Filtration is now the area representative for Carlson Filtration. As a division of Filtrox, Carleson is well know as the premier supplier of filter pads to the beer and wine industry. Carlson has assisted brewers and wine makers in producing fine beverages for over 100 years.

Experience

The principles of Core have over 35 years experience in the design, manufacturing and marketing of filtration and screening products to the process industries.
